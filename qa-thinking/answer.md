## Question 2: Which tests would you NOT automate, and why?

### Tests That Should Remain Manual:

**1. Visual/UI/UX Testing**
- **Why:** Automation can verify elements exist, but cannot judge aesthetic quality, alignment perfection, or user experience feel

**2. Exploratory Testing**
- **Why:** Requires human creativity, intuition, and contextual thinking

**3. Usability & Accessibility Testing**
- **Why:** Requires human judgment and empathy

**4. Tests Requiring Physical Interaction**
- **Why:** Cannot be automated (or extremely difficult/expensive)
